THE LAST AXIOM
Book 1 - Finch's Raven
THE UNIVERSE HAS A TYPO. And Professor Alistair Finch just found the correction pen.
Hidden within the data of a CERN particle collision lies a sequence that shouldn't exist. It isn't an error—it's a message. When Finch dies under mysterious circumstances, his protégé Derek Devon inherits a silver Zippo lighter, a hard drive full of impossible data, and the knowledge that the constants of our universe are being quietly rewritten.
From Cambridge to the Atacama Desert, Derek must decipher the Finch Protocol before the edit is complete. Some believe the universe speaks only in mathematics—but what if we've been listening to only half the conversation?

Derek Devon documents the shifting parameters of reality through his acclaimed Last Axiom series. This is his first novel in a 17-book series exploring the boundaries between science and the inexplicable.
